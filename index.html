The Territory

The Fómhar Ranch sits forty miles outside of Rydeshill, isolated by dense, ancient woods to the west and rolling fields to the east.

  • The Farmhouse: An austere two-story structure with a hidden, tile-lined basement used for the "harvest."
  • The Barn: A massive rust-red structure that houses livestock on the surface and industrial cold storage for human remains in secret.
  • The Perimeter: Guarded by powerfully built dogs and subtle tripwire alarms.
